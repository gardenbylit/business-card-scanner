# Privacy Policy - Business Card Scanner

**Last Updated:** July 6, 2026

## Introduction

Business Card Scanner ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Personal Information You Provide
- **Contact Information:** When you create an account or save business cards, we collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, and company information from scanned business cards.
- **Account Information:** Username, password, profile information, and preferences.
- **Camera Data:** Photos of business cards captured through the app's camera feature.
- **Device Information:** Device model, operating system, unique device identifiers, mobile network information.

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage Data:** Features accessed, frequency of use, actions taken within the app.
- **Camera/Location Permissions:** We request camera permission to scan business cards. We do NOT collect location data unless explicitly enabled by you.
- **Analytics:** Non-personally identifiable usage statistics to improve app performance.

### Third-Party Data
- **Firebase:** User authentication, database synchronization.
- **Realm Database:** Local data storage on your device.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Scan and digitize business cards
- Store and organize your contact database
- Provide cloud synchronization
- Send you service-related notifications
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Comply with legal obligations
- Detect and prevent fraud or security issues

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We **DO NOT** sell your personal data to third parties.

We may share information with:
- **Service Providers:** Firebase, Analytics services (for app improvement only)
- **Legal Requirements:** Law enforcement if required by law
- **Your Consent:** Only with your explicit permission

## 4. Data Security

We implement security measures including:
- End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
- Secure authentication protocols
- Regular security audits
- Secure HTTPS connections

**Note:** No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 5. Data Retention

- **Contact Data:** Retained until you delete it or close your account
- **Account Information:** Retained for your account lifetime + 30 days after deletion
- **Analytics Data:** Retained for 12 months then automatically deleted
- **Cache/Temporary Files:** Automatically cleared when app is uninstalled
